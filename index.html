Our Mission
is to prevent effects of bullying in U.S. middle students.
How it Works
1
Submission
Students uploads their assignments and teachers are able to easily grade, provide feedback, and track various metrics over time like attendance, grades, and participation.
2
Sentiment Analysis
Using Artificial Intelligence technology to analyze the sentiment behind students' creative and written assignments we can identify students needs for support on the basis of bullying or tendency to bully.
3
Empowerment
At risk students are then assigned to courses and outdoor group activities focused on eliminating the factors that are attributed to their risk of bullying and to bully.
